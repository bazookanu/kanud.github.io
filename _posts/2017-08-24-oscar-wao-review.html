The book by Junot Diaz had wicked clever writing and one of the best endings I have read. The meaning of the ending seemed utterly obvious to me[]. The trouble is, people espouse other, utterly obvious interpretations. Diaz's taunting, metaphorical references to "blank pages" further complicate the matter. Here goes my analysis. As a rebellion against my grad school days of the very recent past, where I had to meticulously reference everything I wrote, I am writing this entirely from memory.
