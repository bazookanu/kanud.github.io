Tejas Pandey

Pandeyyyyyyyyy is one of my favourite people on campus ! His idiosyncrasies make you want to talk to him more and more ! An ardent follower and connoisseur of drama , literature and trivia, he has not only entertained people with those perfect acting skills on stage but also otherwise no one can exchange pleasantries the way he does ! An eccentric gujju ( sometimes you feel like he resembles the lad from jamnagar and yet manages to go out with the most sophisticated gentle lady on campus :P) He is got a wide reach from dalal street to the Ambani Bens ! The trading dark knight hidden in him is preparing for the rum to benefit from the bull -bear fight and seek the mulla in style ! Very entertaining , helpful , humble and happy go lucky person ... i am sure he will go a long way in life and success no doubt will come his way... Hope to bump into you sometimes in Bombay !
- Gauri M Mangalgiri
